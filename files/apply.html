New Students

Complete the application and enroll your child for the first time.

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one monthâ€™s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

You may fill out an Application for Admission online or print out and return the pdf version.

  1. Once accepted into the school, fill out the Developmental History Form (only needs to be done once).
  2. Complete all the forms in the "Returning Students" section.

Returning Students

Re-enroll your child.

You must fill out these forms every year that your child attends WUNS.

  • Enrollment Form
  • Child Medical Examination Report (only available in PDF)
  • Immunization Consent/History (only available in PDF)
